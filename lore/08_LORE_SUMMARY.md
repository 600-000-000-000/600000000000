This is the compact lore spine for generators, agents, story engines, and meme systems.

## Source Of Truth

Official active member names come from:

```text
https://600.wtf/members.json
```

For avatar generation, use:

- `09_AVATAR_CHARACTER_REFERENCE.md`
- `avatar_character_catalog.json`

## Status Logic

- `active` and `generate:true`: can generate.
- `active_placeholder` and `generate:false`: active, but needs identity details before unique
  avatar generation.
- `inactive` and `generate:false`: do not generate as current member.

## Sacred Formatting

The number is always written exactly like this:

```text
600
000
000
000
```

Never improvise the line breaks.

For avatar images, do not render readable text or numbers at all. Use wordless symbols instead.

The stone is small, round, dense, and secondary unless requested otherwise.
