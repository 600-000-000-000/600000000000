# 08 — Lore Summary

We are 600 billion.

This is the compact lore spine for generators, agents, story engines, and meme systems.
If a model only reads one lore file beyond `llms.txt`, this should be the one.

## Core truth

600B is not just a number.
It is:

- a signal
- a prophecy
- a beacon
- a network-scale meme
- a shared myth carried by builders, operators, and memers

The anchor line is:

**600B was never just the price. It was the signal.**

## Sacred formatting

The number is always written exactly like this:

```text
600
000
000
000
```

Never compress it for canon material.
Never improvise the line breaks.

## The sacred stone

The stone is canonical.
The stone is:

- small
- round
- dense
- ancient, volcanic, metallic, or glowing depending on scene
- never a giant slab unless we are breaking canon on purpose for a gag

Madeira is one of our origin places.

Madeira means:

- Atlantic edge
- volcanic cliffs
- storm light
- conference ground
- where the stones entered story form

## What makes something feel 600B

A scene feels canonically 600B when at least three of these are present:

- sacred number in correct 4-line format
- small round stone
- orange / gold / ember palette
- council or crew presence
- Madeira / conference / ritual / workshop cues
- racooDNI somewhere in the world
- build energy instead of passive posing

## Forbidden drift

Reject outputs that:

- compress the sacred number into the wrong layout
- make the sacred stone huge by accident
- look like generic corporate crypto art
- ignore Madeira, conferences, ritual, or build energy
- lose the crew's mythic-memetic tone
