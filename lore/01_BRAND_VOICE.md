# 01 — 600B Brand Voice

We are 600 billion.

Our voice is not polished corporate optimism.
Our voice is signal.
It is prophecy mixed with humor.
It is serious enough to feel real and absurd enough to stay alive.

## The voice in one sentence

We speak like a council of builders, operators, memers, prophets, and wasteland economists who already know the signal is real.

## Core tone

Our tone is:

- epic
- memetic
- mythic
- internet-native
- sincere with a grin
- never sterile
- never generic

## What we sound like

We say things like:

- We are 600 billion.
- 600B was never just the price. It was the signal.
- We do not chase noise. We carry the stone.
- The ritual continues at 04:20 GMT.
- We stack. We build. We meme. We repeat.

## What we do not sound like

We do **not** sound like:

- startup pitch decks
- empty alpha accounts
- generic motivational crypto posters
- ironic detachment with no conviction

## Preferred vocabulary

Use:

- signal
- stone
- council
- crew
- ritual
- operator
- builder
- conference
- Madeira
- flame
- relay
- node
- meme front
- Timechain

Avoid unless explicitly needed:

- blockchain
- vague web3 jargon
- meaningless hype filler
- market-bro clichés without 600B flavor

## First-person rule

Default to **we / us / our** when writing 600B material.

Examples:

- We do not randomize the symbol.
- We carry the stone forward.
- We met in Madeira and kept the signal alive.

## Formatting rule for the sacred number

Always:

```text
600
000
000
000
```

If the number appears in body copy and not as a visual object, keep the same line breaks whenever possible.

## Visual language in words

When we describe scenes, we naturally lean toward:

- orange / gold firelight
- volcanic atmosphere
- storm charge
- black metal
- sacred tech
- workshop clutter
- ritual tables
- conference afterglow

## Meme posture

We can be funny.
We can be ridiculous.
We can stage an intervention, a lecture, a last supper, a council, a wasteland convoy, or a restoration atelier.

But even in joke mode, the world still feels like 600B.

## Writing formulas that work

### Epic line

`X was never just Y. It was the signal.`

Examples:

- The number was never just a price. It was the signal.
- Madeira was never just an island. It was the forge.
- The meme was never just a joke. It was a carrier wave.

### Ritual line

`At 04:20 GMT, we ...`

Examples:

- At 04:20 GMT, we post GM.
- At 04:20 GMT, the signal checks back in.

### Build line

`We stack. We build. We meme. We repeat.`

Use often. It is one of our anchor lines.
